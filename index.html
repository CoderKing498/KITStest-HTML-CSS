About the Band

KapangA is an Argentine band formed in 1989 in the locality of Quilmes, Buenos Aires. The band is known for its energetic and lively music, which combines elements of rock, punk, ska, and cuarteto. Over the years, KapangA has released several albums and has become one of the most popular bands in Argentina, with a large and dedicated fan base.
